My name is Cat Noone and I’m the Co-Founder and CEO of Iris, your modern day emergency alert. As a designer, I strive to create products that make a difference and people love. I created Fluid for Sketch, The Gentle Hound, Toothpaste and previously co-founded and was Chief Design Officer for Liberio.

Together with my husband and partner in crime Benedikt Lehnert, I advise startups and invest in them—if they have at least a designer co-founder. If you think we can provide any value to your product and company, send an email my way!

You can find me chatting on Twitter, Writing about design and startups, sharing some designs on Dribbble and ocassionally speaking at conferences.
